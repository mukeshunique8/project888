Abhinav Ramani

Chief Operating Officer (COO)

Abhinav Ramani serves as the Chief Operating Officer (COO) for India at Focus Edumatics Pvt Ltd. He brings over a decade of experience building early-stage medical device companies and driving Fortune 200 healthcare services, working on multiple global, cross-functional teams. Most recently, Abhinav was the Regional Operations Director in Los Angeles and the General Regional Operations Director for the Pacific Northwest at DaVita. In these roles, Abhinav successfully managed a P&L of over $175 million and led a diverse team of over 3,000 talented frontline and middle managers. Abhinav's academic accomplishments include bachelor's and master's degrees in mechanical engineering from Stanford University and an MBA from Harvard Business School. Abhinav had a successful NCAA athletic career that has greatly influenced his commitment to serving others. As a former collegiate gymnast, Abhinav experienced firsthand the transformative power of dedication, discipline, and teamwork. This athletic background instilled in him a deep appreciation for the importance of mentorship, growth, and empowering individuals to reach their highest potential. Deeply committed to authentic leadership and the transformative power of purpose, Abhinav brings unwavering dedication to narrowing the educational equity gap and empowering students throughout the United States to realize their fullest potential. His passion and expertise fuel his efforts to drive Focus Edumatics’ s mission forward!
